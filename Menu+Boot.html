Facebook

Meta is a social media giant known for its flagship platform, Facebook, connecting billions of users worldwide. Beyond social networking, Meta is involved in virtual reality and augmented reality technologies, aiming to redefine the future of digital interaction

Amazon

Amazon is an e-commerce behemoth that revolutionized online shopping, offering a vast selection of products delivered quickly. With ventures into cloud computing (Amazon Web Services), streaming services, and artificial intelligence (Alexa), Amazon has diversified its portfolio to become a global tech giant

Apple

Apple is renowned for its innovative hardware and software products, including the iPhone, iPad, Mac, and iOS ecosystem. The company's commitment to design, user experience, and ecosystem integration has made it a leader in consumer electronics and software development

Netflix

Netflix has transformed the entertainment industry with its subscription-based streaming service, offering a vast library of movies, TV shows, and original content. It popularized the binge-watching culture and became a major disruptor in the traditional television landscape

Google

Google is synonymous with online search but has expanded its reach into various technologies. As part of Alphabet Inc., Google is involved in internet-related services, advertising, cloud computing (Google Cloud), autonomous vehicles (Waymo), and cutting-edge projects like artificial intelligence and healthcare innovations
